School history
Card subtitle

Merriment School And Collage was established in 1885. The school was in a small house south of the Gaibandha main post office. The house which was donated by the then zamindar of Bamondanga, scholar Swargiya Sarat Chandra Roy Chowdhury Mahashay Zamindar Babu's land in the school is 6 acres 68 percent. The main school building was constructed with public subscriptions and donations due to the tireless efforts of the then Rangpur District Commissioner JN Das Gupta and Gaibandha Sub-Divisional Commissioner JC Dutt.
